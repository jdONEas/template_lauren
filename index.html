Seção Sobre





Born and raised in Miami, Lauren Jauregui is a Cuban-American singer,
songwriter & performing artist who initially rose to fame as a member of the multi-platinum award-winning girl group, Fifth Harmony.
E mbarking on her solo career in 2018, Lauren began her process of self-discovery as both an artist and woman after the group’s hiatus and went on to collaborate
with various artists including Halsey on their queer anthem “Strangers” and Steve Aoki on their electronic banger “All Night”.
While signed to Columbia Records, she released a series of singles including her debut “Expectations,” which has surpassed 100 million streams, and “Lento” featuring renowned Grammy award-winning producer Tainy and later remixed by Rauw Alejandro and Pablo Vittar. She also contributed
“Invisible Chains” to the Brirds of Prey soundtrack, and “While I’m Alive” to the first volume of Femme It Forward, an all-women compilation album in 2021.
